//    The four elements performance

The Four Elements is a multi-arts performance that brings together Music (for Piano and Cello), Dance, Paintings, Poems and ColorFlow.

It started being developed by pianist Teija Englund, composer Luis Pato and artist Meinert Steensen in Autumn 2011 and it was first performed in Sylvia-Koti, Finland, in August 2012.

The Four Elements is divided in 4 movements, one for each of the elements, each one with its own rich character and mood.
